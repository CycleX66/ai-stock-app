How CycleX Works

CycleX is designed as a continuously updating, AI-driven, risk-adaptive market intelligence portal. It identifies and ranks opportunities by combining market behaviour, company quality, news and event context, macroeconomic backdrop, and risk-band filtering.

Market & Technical Layer

CycleX evaluates price behaviour, trend structure, volatility, and relative ranking within the screened universe.

Company Fundamentals

CycleX is designed to assess business quality, earnings strength, revenue durability, and balance-sheet resilience.

News & Event Intelligence

CycleX is intended to incorporate breaking company developments, earnings updates, sentiment shifts and material event risk.

Macro & Geopolitical Context

CycleX is built to consider inflation, rates, policy decisions, currencies and wider geopolitical developments affecting markets.

Risk-Adaptive Filtering

Outputs are filtered through Defensive, Cautious, Balanced, Growth or Aggressive profiles so the surfaced set matches the selected risk posture.

Real-Time Layer

CycleX is designed to work as a continuously updating engine, dynamically reassessing and re-ranking opportunities as conditions evolve.

Data inputs may include a combination of real-time, delayed and periodically refreshed sources depending on market availability and system configuration.
